The home ranch

The Outfit

A family camp on a 6,000-acre Wyoming ranch.

CK Hunting is a family run camp on a 6,000-acre working ranch in the Wyoming Range, run by Ross and Deena Copeland. We are second-generation outfitters with more than 30 years of guiding experience, and we build our seasons around archery and rifle hunts for elk, mule deer, moose, and antelope.

The ranch was homesteaded in the early 1900s, and the camp was started in 1980. It borders four miles of the Bridger-Teton National Forest and one of Wyoming's elk feedgrounds, so we hunt the ranch, the national forest, and some of the surrounding BLM. Camp sits on North Cottonwood Creek, 20 miles west of Daniel, Wyoming.

North Cottonwood Creek

Camp & Ranch

A drive-in camp on North Cottonwood Creek.

Three smaller cabins for our hunters and one large cabin where we cook family-style meals and keep a full bathroom. Rustic, but comfortable.

Wildlife is frequently seen right around camp, and the creek runs just steps from your cabin door for relaxing trout fishing at the end of the day.

  • Three guest cabins and a cook cabin
  • Family-style meals and a full bathroom
  • Trout fishing steps from your door
  • 20 miles west of Daniel, Wyoming

After the Hunt

Trout on the creek, right outside your cabin.

North Cottonwood Creek runs through camp, just steps from the cabins. It is part of why we call this a hunting and fishing camp. Bring a rod, and end the day on the water.

How to book, and how to get a tag.
